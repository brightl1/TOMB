Welcome to TOMB: The Online Map of Bioarchaeology

TOMB is a digital cultural map of bioarchaeological research sites around the world. This is an educational tool highlighting the breadth of current bioarchaeological research.

Hovering over the map icons will make a short info window appear. Clicking on the map icons will bring up more information on that site. Clicking the blue "Site" link will direct you to the full page for each site.
